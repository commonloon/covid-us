Until December 14 of 2020, the European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control (ECDC) provided daily COVID-19 new case data. After Dec 14, they switched to providing only 14 day averages, updated weekly.

As this information is not useful for visualizing current trends, I did not update my scripts.
